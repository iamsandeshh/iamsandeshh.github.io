IMPACTS oF TECHNOLOGY IN OUR SOCIETY


Introduction

Technology refers to method, system and devices which are the result of scientific knowledge being used for practical purposes. It is a knowledge of science that deals with creation and use of technical means and their interrelation with life, society and the environment. In this 2st century human beings are fully dependent on technology. We use technology to run our daily activities. Technology help us to fulfil our basic requirement and desire. Technology can be used for both positive and negative aspect. It has made great impact in the various things but one of the main affect is in our daily life.


Advantages of technology

As we know 21st century is an era of science and technology, we are surrounded by different technology. Technology has become a part of our life. Modern technology make works easier, faster and convenient. Technology has made word smaller. We can travel world from one corner to another. With help of internet we can easily communicate with the people from the globe. Internet has made the world social village, we can get information from all around the globe on the internet. Technology has truly resulted in digitalization and moderation in many fields like medicine, farming, internet banking, industries and much more. Technology has resulted in a globe revolution.

Disadvantage of technology

Not only positive, Technology has negative impact also. Technology has become a threats to human. Many countries are building atomic bombs and nuclear weapons for defending a country but these invention can destroy humankind. Due to technology it has increase unemployment problem. More machine and tools are inventing day by day which mean less labor and employees.
